Welcome to our mini hotel Avenue in the very center of the city.

Interior

Interior inside Avenue designed in pleasant tints creates an atmosphere of warmness and light which makes anyone to feel at ease. Each room is individually designed and has its own unique style. Comfort simplicity and modernity so important for business people, travelers, families with children and other categories of our guests have been successfully matched here inside the Avenue.

Room Info

We offer to our customers comfortable apartments of different categories, namely, standard, superiors, and deluxe suites where everything is functional so that a guest can rest mentally and physically.
Modern and cozy furniture, TV with distance control, bathroom unit with a toilet, sink, shower box and hair dryer – all is aimed at making your stay a most pleasant one.

A Tea Room

The hotel features a comfortable tea room where you can enjoy drinking a cup of tea or coffee, looking through press, working on some documents or papers, and just having a good time in a company of interesting people.
