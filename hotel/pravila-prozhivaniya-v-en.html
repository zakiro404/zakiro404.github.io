RULES OF STAY IN THE AVENUE HOTEL.

Dear Sirs and Madams!
Here below there’s a summary of rules of stay in the AVENUE HOTEL:

1. The AVENUE HOTEL is intended for temporary living of customers for time agreed with the hotel administration. On the expiry of the term agreed a customer is to clear the room on demand of the administration. If a customer wishes to extend the term of living the hotel administration must be informed about that no later than 2 hours before check-out or 12 hours by local time. Extension of the term of living just in the same room is possible only if that hasn’t been reserved on behalf of the third party.

2. Pay hour is at 12:00 by the local time. Accommodation of guests starts at 1 p.m. or at 13:00 by the local time.

3. Operating hours – round-the-clock schedule.

4. Accommodation of guests in the hotel rooms is to be carried out upon presentation of identity documents, such as passport, military ID, etc. A guest fills in a registration card, certifying that the information about himself (or herself) is correct and true and that has nothing against the rules of staying.

5. The hotel administration is entitled to conclude contracts for room reservation. Upon having free rooms the Administration takes the booking requests from legal and natural persons by telephones, post office, and other means of communication.

6. When reserving or accommodating or free resettling a customer has the right to choose a category of the room he’d like to have, but the role of choosing the room of this category rightfully belongs to the hotel Administration. In case of non-arrival charge applies for keeping reservation but no more than for one day. In case it’s late for more than one day the reservation is to be cancelled. Room reservation can be available from 14:00 up to 12:00 of the following day i.e. pay hour.

7. Pay for stay and services in the hotel is due to be done in accordance with the price list approved by the hotel administration.

8. Children up to 7 years old without providing a separate space are not charged for accommodation.

9. Room reservation is not charged.

10. In case of refusal or shortening of the stay period the hotel reserves the right to keep the daily value from the full payment of the reserved rooms. The amount a customer (a guarantor) has paid in advance is considered to be done for the room a customer has reserved under his name for the whole period of his stay.

11. Change of the bed –clothes is provided every third day, whilst change of towels and room cleaning are provided every day. Change of toiletries (soap, shampoo, shower gel, toilet paper) is provided as needed during the stay. The AVENUE HOTEL provides its guests with the following free services:

12. The hotel delivers additional services paid at the guest’s wish in accordance with the price list for the hotel’s additional services.

13. The hotel is not liable for the city services operation (electricity and heat blockouts, disconnection of water supply)

14. The hotel guest is liable:

- to turn the taps off and close the windows
- to follow the rules of stay in the hotel
- to maintain cleanliness, silence and social order inside the room and hotel
- to strictly follow fire safety regulations
- to compensate any damage or deterioration (loss, shortage, and breakage of property belonging to the hotel)
- to be responsible for actions of people invited by the customer to the room
- to pay on time and fully for all the additional services provided in the hotel

15. In the hotel it is forbidden:

- To leave strangers in the room and give them the key of the room
- To keep huge things, flammable materials, weapons, chemical and radioactive substances, mercury
- To keep animals in the room
- To smoke in non-smoking rooms, in halls, and corridors of the hotel
- To be in alcoholic and drug haze
- To use heating devices in the room if it is not allowed by the hotel rules
- To disturb the carm of neighbors

16. A customer agrees to pay a fine for smoking in a non-smoking room in amount of 1000 soms which is supposed to be spent to purify the room air from smell of smoke.

17. The hotel administration is not liable for the loss of the customer’s values and properties kept in the room when he breaks the rules of stay. In case of finding things lost the hotel administration takes actions to send back those to the owners. If the owner is not found the hotel administration applies to the police or to the local city administration.

18. The hotel administration reserves the right to visit the room without the customer’s agreement in case of haze and fire episodes, flooding, and also in case if a guest breaks the rules of stay and public order as well as the rules of using home appliances.

19. The hotel is free either to evict the violent guest or to refuse in extension of the stay period if there’s a violation of any payment obligations, order of staying in the hotel, and if the guest refuses to compensate damages.

20. In case the hotel administration receives complaints from the guest it is to takes all possible measures to regulate the conflict.
